About COPEVID-19

COPEVID-19 is an effort to help people struggling due to the COVID-19 pandemic using tools and technology.

The Supply Tracker

The COPEVID-19 supply tracker is our first application. It was built to help our communities find essential items during shortages from supply chain disruptions and panic buying. Ordering from online retailers or relying on store websites for an accurate account of the inventory has become less reliable during the periods of unpredictable buying and high demand. The supply tracker aims to inform the community about what is actually on the shelf of their local stores through the power of crowd sourcing and collaboration. Neighbors shopping for items at local stores can use the app to easily mark which essential items are in stock, limited stock, or out of stock. The application then presents a catalog of items and stores with the latest inventory status as confirmed by the community. The catalog is updated in real time and can be used to see which locations have a particular item in stock or what is the status of different essential items at a particular store. The application also tracks the history of updates for an item at a store in order to detect a pattern for when an item is most likely to be in stock.
